Quick tips

  • Sessions keep tab titles and URLs in the original order.
  • Use the limit in Settings to avoid clutter.
  • Moving to a new computer? Export → Import.

Get started

  1. Pin the extension and open the popup.
  2. Press Save tabs to store the current window.
  3. Use the ⋮ menu to rename, restore, preview or delete.
  4. Open Settings to back up or restore via JSON.

Privacy

Session data is stored locally using chrome.storage.local. No URLs or preferences are sent to external servers.
